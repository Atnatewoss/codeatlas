CodeAtlas is a deep research chatbot designed for engineering teams navigating complex open-source software. By leveraging **Tree of Thought** reasoning and **LangGraph** orchestration, it autonomously analyzes repository structure, explores architectural perspectives, and evaluates design decisions. 

The result? Comprehensive research reports and architecture visualizations that help engineers rapidly comprehend, adapt, and scale large-scale codebases.

## Features

- **Deep Codebase Analysis:** Maps out intricate repository structures and surfaces implicit design patterns.
- **Tree of Thought Reasoning:** Explores multiple architectural hypotheses to evaluate complex engineering decisions.
- **Automated Research Reports:** Generates in-depth documentation and research summaries on-demand.
- **Visual Architecture Mapping:** Creates clear, actionable visualizations of system components and data flows.
